Long Drive Rules


No TopGolf in the Texas Hill Country, so we're just going to launch golf balls out into a field. Fairway indicators will be setup, which ball must land between in order to be considered. Furthest distance including roll gets 1st place, next furthest 2nd, etc. Each player gets 5 swings and takes best single ball.

SNAFU

Nothing in fairway - chug a beer

SAVIOR

Call 1 of the 5 shots as money ball - if that is your scoring ball, you get an extra point on the scoreboard for this event
