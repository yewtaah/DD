Kan Jam Rules


Pair play at 30 foot distance between the cans will decide who gets bragging rights for Kan Jam. One player throws the disc at the kan while their partner attempts to direct it in.

  • 1 point for deflections that touch the kan
  • 2 points for direct touch without partner help
  • 3 point for deflections into the kan
  • shot into coin slot or top unassisted is immediate win
Teams alternate going both directions for as many rounds as necessary. Game is played to 21 points with no penalty for going over. A single elimination tournament format will be used.

SNAFU

No points in a given round mean that team has to finish both their drinks

SAVIOR

As soon as 13 combined points are reached by both teams, next throw is preceeded by a football toss at the kan. Make it in - walk off win!
