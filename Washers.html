Washers Rules


Game of washers will be played to 21 points with each player standing next to the box without stepping in front of it. Net scoring is used so if one team scores 4 points while their opponents have 1 point, 3 net (4-1) points are awarded. To win, a team must score exactly 21 points. If you bust, you go back to 11.

SNAFU

Miss the board with all washers 2 rounds in a row and it will cost you 10 push ups

SAVIOR

Each team gets one veto where they can reject a washer throw before it is made. This can be done at any time and the opposing team must surrender that one washer.
