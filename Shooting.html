Shooting Rules


Who doesn't love those classic shooting galleries? We're recreating the experience by placing clay targets in trees and shooting them out with a .22! 10 shots for each player - any piece of the target broken off is counted as 1 point. Ties will be decided with a sudden death shootout.

SNAFU

A score of 3 or less earns you a shot of liquor

SAVIOR

1 bonus disc will exist in each set of 10 - hit it for an extra shooting gallery point
