Skeet Shooting Rules

What better way to kickoff the tournament than blasting off some shells with a shotgun! This year we'll be on private property, so will use a manual pull thrower to send 25 clay pigeons into the Texas sky. Each player will get 25 shots and tabulate the number of hits (partial or dusted).

SNAFU

The lurking penalty at skeet will be imposed on anyone hitting less than 10 targets: shotgun a beer

SAVIOR

Each player will roll a singe dice before shooting. The number that comes up determines the lucky pull. If the player smashes the number target (3 on dice means 3rd target) play will receive an extra point on their final ranking score.
